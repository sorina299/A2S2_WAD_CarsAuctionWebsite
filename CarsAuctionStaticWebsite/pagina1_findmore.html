AuctionGarage is an online auction platform for vehicles. Count on us for the daily delivery of over 2,000 high quality cars, each with a very detailed description. Once you have registered, you have access to full descriptions and car prices. And the most importantly, you can start placing bids.

Quality vehicles, quality descriptions


It would be easy to say that we are bidding on used cars, damaged cars, vans and light trucks. But we do much more. We offer you peace of mind. Our descriptions include full details of the vehicle, damage reports and a complete photo gallery with images of the vehicle, as well as photographs of any damage. For many vehicles we even have external inspection reports. It gives you that very important sense of peace.

What if the car has a problem? You don't have to worry. We have developed a policy for resolving complaints that guarantees fast and correct resolution. You can find the full complaint handling policy in your account. Please be sure to read it before you start bidding!
